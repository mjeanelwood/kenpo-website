Women's Self Defense

What is Self Defense? Self-defense is a set of skills and techniques that enable someone to successfully escape, resist, and/or survive violent attacks. A good self-defense course provides psychological awareness and verbal skills, not just physical training. The right of self-defense is the right for people to use reasonable force or defensive force, for the purpose of defending one's own life or the lives of others. Self-defense is for everyone and can be of great benefit when taught responsibly. Knowing how to defend yourself can boost your confidence, reduce anxiety and make you more aware of your surroundings. For more information contact us for a free personal assessment.
